Laegna math operations in it's flexible syntax - it's a little clearer to do arithmetics with operations themselves, because the meanings of things are aligned.

Notice you can use standard math operations or their Laegna-enchanced version, instead of Laegna operators - use them where they make sense, such as making clear and vivid which numbers they represent, which is my common use - I use them for Laegna-basic, and Latin ones for operations I would do in Latin; most commonly - Laegna, in my hands, tends to be strictly Laegna, for example *never* inversing the negative in any way unless explicit etc.

These operators are optional, because you can use latin equivalents as in this example:
- https://logex-life.catdoes.app/syntax
